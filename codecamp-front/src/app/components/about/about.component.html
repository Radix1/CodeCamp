Code Camp was the brainchild of four students of Techno Main Salt Lake.
As a matter of fact, all four of them started their careers under the umbrella of Tata Consultancy Services, and within a year they had attained great heights and moved on to work at even greater places.
Codecamp had by far remained the most sought after platform to host coding competitions during EDGE, the techno management fest of Techno Main Salt Lake.
All the CUrBrain members will remain forever grateful to the creators of CodeCamp, who had opened the world of coding for them and helped them embark on this journey.
